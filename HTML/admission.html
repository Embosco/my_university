About mbosco University Admissions

Mbosco University admits talented students globally. Our admissions process evaluates academics, leadership, extracurriculars, and personal qualities. We select students who will thrive and contribute positively.

Admission Requirements

  • High School Diploma or equivalent
  • Standardized Test Scores (SAT/ACT optional)
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Personal Essay
  • Extracurricular Activities
  • Passport or National ID
  • Recent Passport Photograph

Important Deadlines

Application Type Deadline
Early Action November 1
Regular Decision January 1

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application fee?

$75, fee waivers available.

Can international students apply?

Yes, we welcome students worldwide.

Do I need standardized test scores?

SAT/ACT scores are optional. Check program requirements.
